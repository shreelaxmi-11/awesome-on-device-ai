# 📓 Chat with Notion Export (Local)

Export your Notion workspace and chat with your own notes privately. No API key, no cloud, your notes stay on your machine.

## How to export from Notion

1. Open Notion Settings → **Settings & members**
2. Click **Export all workspace content**
3. Format: **Markdown & CSV**
4. Download and unzip
